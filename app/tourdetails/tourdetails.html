Full Description

University and inner city park, known as The Domain. Continue on through the picturesque suburb of Ponsonby, known for its historic buildings, before heading over the Harbour Bridge to the North Shore. Take a stroll on Narrow Neck Beach in historic Devonport before continuing up to the summit of Mt Victoria for panoramic views of Auckland city and the Harbour. Next you will visit the beautiful Viaduct Harbour and Westhaven Marina, one of the largest marinas in the Southern Hemisphere, before leaving the city behind and heading west to Kumeu Wine Country where you will enjoy wine tastings followed by lunch at a contemporary vineyard restaurant.
