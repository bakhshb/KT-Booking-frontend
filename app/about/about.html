Welcome aboard! - Meet the Kiwiana Team

To kiwianatours online booking

Kiwiana is owned and operated by local New Zealanders Dave Ovenden and Catherine Kirk who are well travelled and whom have hosted international travellers from all over the world for the past 15 years. All of our tours are guided by people who can help you find what you are looking for and understand the needs of the local and international traveller. They are on hand to provide sightseeing and commentary on things of interest, and generally help you find amazing things on and off the beaten track. You are free to use them as much or as little as you like.

Jobs at Kiwiana Tours

We often have jobs available if you are interested in becoming a part of the Kiwiana team and show many international travellers our beautiful country.

We are currently recruiting driver guides on call for our summer season and beyond. Please send your CV to info@kiwianatours.com.

You are a university student from New Zealand or overseas and looking for an internship in tourism? We from time to time have positions available - please send your CV to info@kiwianatours.com. We will reply within 7 days.
